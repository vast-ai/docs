## What payout methods does [Vast.ai](http://Vast.ai) support?

<Note>
  **V[ast.ai](http://ast.ai) currently supports payouts via Stripe, PayPal, and Wise <u>only.</u>**
</Note>

Providers are responsible for ensuring they can receive payments for services from a business  (not peer-to-peer payments) through one of the supported payout methods listed above and that their account is fully set up and compliant (e.g., identity verification, business information).

<Warning>
  If you are unable to receive payouts due to geographic restrictions, regulatory requirements, or issues with your payout account, [Vast.ai](http://Vast.ai) is not able to issue payments through alternative methods. You will need to resolve any issues directly with your selected payout provider.
</Warning>

<Danger>
  If you are unsure whether you can receive payouts, please confirm with the payout provider before listing hardware.
</Danger>

## When will I get paid?

Your first payout typically takes about **2 weeks**.

Here’s how it works:

- Once your machines generate earnings and reach at least **\$20**, an invoice will be created on the next **Friday**.
- That invoice will remain in a **“Pending”** status for one week.
- The payout is then processed on the **following Friday**.

After your first payout, this process continues on a weekly cycle.

<Tip>
  If you have never received a payout before, and you haven't received the funds after your invoice was sent for payment, ensure your account with the payout provider (Paypal, Stripe, Wise) is in good standing before reaching out to us. This will expedite your resolution.

  Depending on your location, you may be required to complete additional verification or provide documentation before funds can be received.
</Tip>

### Why does my invoice show “Paid” if I haven’t received the money yet?

The **“Paid”** status means that [Vast.ai](http://Vast.ai) has submitted the payment to your selected payout provider (e.g., PayPal, Stripe, or Wise).

It does **not** mean the funds have already arrived in your account.

Once the payment is sent, it may take additional time for the payout provider to process and deliver the funds. Timing can vary depending on the provider, your account status, and any required verification steps.

### Can I generate an invoice?

Yes. You can generate an invoice from the **Earnings** page.

1. Go to **Earnings** → **Payout History**
2. Select the date range you want to include
3. Export the data as a **CSV or PDF**
